Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a delicious and healthy dip that's perfect for parties or snacking. It's made with ripe avocados, tomatoes, onions, and lime juice. Follow the steps below to make your own guacamole:

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Lime juice
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Jalapeno (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
  2. Scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl and mash it with a fork.
  3. Dice the tomatoes, onions, and cilantro, and add them to the bowl.
  4. Add lime juice, salt, and garlic powder to taste.
  5. If desired, finely chop a jalapeno and add it to the guacamole for some heat.
  6. Stir everything together until well combined.
  7. Adjust seasonings to taste, then serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, burritos, or salads.
