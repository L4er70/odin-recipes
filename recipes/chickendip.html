Chicken Dip

Description

Chicken dip is a delicious appetizer that's perfect for parties or gatherings. It's creamy, cheesy, and packed with flavor. Follow the steps below to make your own chicken dip:

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Hot sauce
  • Ranch dressing
  • Green onions
  • Tortilla chips (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook and shred the chicken.
  2. Mix together the cream cheese, cheddar cheese, hot sauce, ranch dressing, and green onions.
  3. Add the shredded chicken to the mixture and stir until well combined.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and bake until bubbly and golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with tortilla chips.
