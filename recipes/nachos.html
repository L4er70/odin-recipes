Nachos

Description

Nachos are a classic Tex-Mex dish that's perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying as a snack. They're easy to make and customizable with your favorite toppings. Follow the steps below to make your own nachos:

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Black beans
  • Jalapenos
  • Tomatoes
  • Green onions
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole

Steps

  1. Spread a layer of tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  2. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the chips.
  3. Add black beans, jalapenos, and diced tomatoes.
  4. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Top with green onions, sour cream, and guacamole.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
